What you need

Two players in different places. Each gets a link to the same game via a room code.

Starting the game

Both players roll the green dice. Whoever scores higher goes first. Ties are rerolled.

Each round

The active player clicks the red master dice. That number decides how many blue dice they may roll. Then pick which blue dice to roll — their total is the round score.

Power-ups — once per game each

🎲 Golden Reroll — Activate on your own turn after rolling the master dice (red). Discard the result and roll a fresh one — you must accept the new value.

🥽 Future Goggles — Peek at one unrolled blue dice to see its value before deciding whether to pick it.

🧿 The Jinx — Activate on your opponent's turn after they roll the master dice. Forces them to roll it again and accept the new result.

🌀 The Scramble — Activate on your opponent's turn after they've rolled blue dice. All their rolled dice get new random values.

Winning

After 3 rounds, whoever has the highest total score wins!
